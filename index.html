Our Story

Inspired by the rich seafood traditions of Vasai, we bring you fresh, bold flavors in a vibrant setting. Located just miles from the Arabian Sea, our restaurant celebrates the diverse culinary heritage of the Konkan coast.

Our chefs source the freshest catch daily, prepare spice blends in-house, and cook with techniques passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of our coastal heritage - from the tangy kokum sol kadhi to the fiery Malvani masalas.

"Our mission is to preserve and share the authentic flavors of our ancestral home while creating a modern dining experience for our guests." - Chef Priya Naik, Founder
